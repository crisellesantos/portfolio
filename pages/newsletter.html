Newsletter

A newsletter is a regularly distributed publication that is generally about one main topic of interest to its subscribers. It is a good tool that contributes to branding and establishes a connection with clients. This is a 4-page newsletter created in InDesign for a local cupcake business. To successfully incorporate the cupcake theme throughout the pages, I made column grids that look like a cupcake and a rolling pin.
