Steampunk Alice

Every year, the San Diego County Fair host competitive exhibits which features student projects from San Diego County. More than 9,000 projects are received, judged and displayed each year. This piece was entered on the 2016 county fair with a theme “Mad About Fair”. I combined the youthfulness of Alice in Wonderland with the ruggedness of steampunk to portray the fair’s theme. California State Senate recognized this illustration as “Best of Class”.
