STEAKHOUSE WEBSITE

This project involves identifying a website that we feel needs a redesign. I chose an old-school steakhouse in San Diego that serves steaks and seafood dishes. This place has an old school charm with great live music and serves classic drinks alongside their generous portions of dishes. However, their website is outdated. It needs updating, it needs to improve usability and responsiveness and incorporate and engage in social media.

For the redesign, the desired outcome is to invite more customers in order to increase revenue. The existing website has loud colors, too many navigation links and unresponsive. I removed unnecessary information and rearranged the main navigation links for the clean, simple look. I also incorporated social media links at the footer of the website.
